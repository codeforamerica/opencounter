Utility Services

You've told us you're working out of a commercial location, which means you'll need utility service.

Gas and Power

In our City, gas and power is supplied by PG&E. You'll have to contact them on your own if your landlord or building owner hasn't set up a separate account and meter for you.

Water, Sewage, and Trash Service

These are all provided by the City of Santa Cruz, and we'll help you get your application in with this guide to save you a trip to City Hall.

For trash service, you must place the container on private property unless street access is permitted by the Public Works Department.
