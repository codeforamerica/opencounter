Parking

Downtown Businesses

Because your business is downtown, you don't need to provide any parking spaces, but you will need to pay a Parking Deficiency Fee every quarter.

These fees, based normally on square footage, are used to maintain City parking lots, build new ones, and also pay for extra public safety patrols, street cleaning and bike lockers.

In 2011, the City created an incentive for new businesses that move downtown that waives these fees for a year, and then discounts them for an additional six months as your businesses grows downtown.

There is no application form for parking fees, as it uses the same inforamtion we ask for on your business license application.
