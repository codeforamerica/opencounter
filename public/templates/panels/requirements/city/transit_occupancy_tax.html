Transit Occupancy Tax

Transit Occupancy Tax (TOT), also known as Hotel Tax exists in paid by the occupant and collected by the operator. The operator then remits the tax to the city.

There is no charge to register to collect this tax.If you’d like to read more about the TOT, click here.
